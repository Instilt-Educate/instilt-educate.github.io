Our Mission

Instilt Educate is a fiscally sponsored 501(c)(3) nonprofit that provides free online tutoring to students from underprivileged backgrounds. The mission of the organization is to give those who may not possess the same opportunities as others more possibilities through quality lessons.

Our Model

In our lessons, we pair one native English-speaking tutor with another proficient in the language of the students. Through this, tutors are able to build a bridge between the languages that students can utilize, allowing them a chance for easy communication and outside exposure with tutors who speak English in their day-to-day life. Students are also able to communicate with tutors well versed in their native languages, maintaining a comfortable and familiar environment.

Our Method

Online experience made interactive

Through our primary learning interface Zoom, we make sure to facilitate an environment where students can interact, collaborate, and inquire comfortably and effectively. Outside of class, we ensure continuous interaction through Whatsapp.

Learning by application

We work to ensure that all lesson plans include a variety of engaging activities that allow students to apply their knowledge in order to strengthen and enhance their understanding.

Cohesive curriculum with a wide scope

Our organization works to ensure that lesson plans are fully comprehensive and include varied content, inspired by reputable sources such as Indian Boards (CBSE), American Schooling Systems, Khan Academy, etc.

Tailored to their learning needs

To ensure the success of the students, we have constructed a flexible curriculum that can be tailored to individual learning needs. With an adaptable approach and action-packed lessons, the students come first — always.
