Our Mission

Instilt Educate is a fiscally sponsored 501(c)(3) nonprofit providing free, quality, online tutoring to students from underprivileged backgrounds. Our mission is to level the playing field between children that come from more fortunate backgrounds and those who do not possess the same privilege.

Our Model

In an effort to grow and deepen the understanding of our students, we use a unique model. For our faculty, we pair one native English speaker with another tutor proficient in the language of the students. This way students are able to enjoy the best of both worlds as they are pushed to communicate seamlessly with a qualified tutor who speaks English in their day-to-day, allowing them some great outside exposure. At the same time, the other tutor is always to ensure that the students are following, maintaining a comfortable and familiar environment for the children.

Our Method

Online experience made interactive

Utilizing Zoom as our primary learning interface, we facilitate an interactive and comfortable environment where students can communicate, collaborate, and inquire effectively, complementing the continuous interaction outside of class through Whatsapp.

Learning by application

As part of our teaching curriculum, we strive to incorporate all content into engaging application-based activities designed to strengthen and enhance the students’ knowledge.

Cohesive curriculum with a wide scope

Our team works diligently to create comprehensive lesson plans inclusive of content inspired by reputed sources: Indian Boards (CBSE), American Schooling Systems, Khan Academy, etc.

Tailored to their learning needs

To ensure the success of the students, we have constructed an incredibly flexible curriculum, adaptable approach, and action-packed lessons suited to meet the needs of the students – always.
